You are always at risk of a fire, even when you dont expect it. Dont worry as this website will teach you how to prevent fires at your house with these simple steps to ensure a fire free home.

1.
Always unplug any device that isnt being used at the moment
2.
Turn off lights when not in use
3.
Keep caution when dealing with hot stuff like stoves
4.
Put anything that is easily flammable away from children
5.
Have a fire extinguisher near any common place where heat is usally envolved incase of an accident
